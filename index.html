Curry Leaf Chicken

The recipe is very simple and delicious.

Chicken Gravy Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 500 grams
  • Curry leaves - a handful
  • Pepper powder - 2 spoons
  • Ginger, garlic paste - 1 spoon

Instructions:

  1. Clean the chicken well.
  2. Add ginger, garlic paste and curry leaves and sauté.
  3. Add chicken, sprinkle pepper powder and cook.
  4. Delicious Curry Leaf Chicken is ready!

Paneer Butter Masal

  • Paneer - 200 grams
  • Onions - 2
  • Tomatoes - 3
  • Cashews - 10
  • Butter - 50 grams
  • Garam Masala - 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Fresh Cream - 2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste

Instructions:

  1. Melt a little butter in a pan, add chopped onions, tomatoes, and cashews, sauté them, let them cool, and grind them into a smooth paste.
  2. Melt the remaining butter in the pan again, and add cumin powder, turmeric powder, and chili powder to sauté.
  3. Add the ground paste and sauté until the oil separates.
  4. Add garam masala, salt, and a little water, and let it simmer.
  5. Finally, add the paneer pieces and fresh cream, cook on low heat for a few minutes, and then remove from heat.
  6. Delicious Paneer Butter Masala is ready!
